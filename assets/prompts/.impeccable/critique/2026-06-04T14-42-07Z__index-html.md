**[P1] Pain + market gap sections gated behind IntersectionObserver** — .pain-reveal and .problem-item ship blank in headless/background-tab contexts. **[P2] Two h1 on one page** — hero h1 at line 1261 + product-flow h1 "Voici Carry IT" at line 1492. Fix: change product-flow h1 to h2. Suggested: /impeccable harden

**[P2] Hero: three text blocks before CTA** — .hero-description + .hero-badge both float between title and CTAs. Badge ("Sans gamification. ## Minor Observations

- font-weight: 650 and 680 used throughout — non-standard, rounds to 600/700. Use standard weights.
- .product-flow-section redefines --font-display and --font-stack, shadowing global tokens.
- manifest-overline is a full sentence in ALL CAPS — violates all-caps body copy rule.
- Scroll indicator arrow at rgba(255,255,255,0.22) ≈ 1.4:1 contrast — reads as broken.
- No back-navigation / anchor links in the page for users who want to jump between sections.
